About Me

Hello! My name is Anjali and welcome to my kitchen! Here you will find recipes from all over the world but I have decided to take a few steps back and take things easy for a little while. In this book, I am going to first introduce easy recipes because this is for all the beginners out there. We are going to begin with pastas, burrito bowls and milkshakes. So come with me and let's get cooking!
