burrito bowl


Ingredients corn, red onion, peppers and more onions, avocado, cilantro, rice, butter, black beans, and lime slices.

First toast the rice for a few minutes. Then add water and let it cook for 10 minutes. Add a couple tablespoons of butter to finish it off and garnish with cilantro. Next, chop up a red onion, and add some corn with a couple chillies to make a cool salsa. Then take 2 avocados, mash them up, put it in a bowl. Add salt, pepper, onion and set off to the side. For the vegetables, make sure to roast and saute them in a pan for about 5-7 minutes. Add garlic salt, chipotle and taco seasoning, some fresh black pepper and it is done. Lastly, the black beans. This is optional but once they are done cooking, you can add cotija cheese and some cilantro on top. Salsa is optional but if you want to make it homemade, take tomatoes, onions, a little bit of lemon juice, and mix it up!
