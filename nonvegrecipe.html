Fettucine Alfredo with Chicken

Ingredients

Fettucine Pasta, Milk, Flour, Nutmeg, Salt, Pepper, Cheese, Chicken Pieces, Oil, Garlic Salt, Italian Seasoning

Method

Boil the pasta for about 5-7 minutes. Then drain it and set it off to the side. In a pan on medium-high heat, throw in some oil, and put in your chicken pieces. WHile they are browning, ass the garic salt and italian seasoning. Put the chicken pieces onto a plate. In another pan, put flour and slowly add your milk until it gets nice and thick, and no lumps are formed. Once the sauce is thick, add the cheese and stir nicely. After that, put the pasta and chicken. Let it cook for a few more minutes. Make sure to add your nutmeg as well.
